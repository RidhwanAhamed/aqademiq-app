We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly:

1. Email us at security@studyplanner.app with details
2. Allow us reasonable time to investigate and address the issue  
3. Do not publicly disclose the vulnerability until we've had a chance to fix it
4. We appreciate responsible disclosure and will acknowledge your contribution

Scope: This policy applies to all StudyPlanner services and web applications.

Out of scope:
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks against our infrastructure
- Denial of service attacks
- Reports of spam or phishing not directly related to our platform

Thank you for helping keep StudyPlanner secure!
